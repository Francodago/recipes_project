Pizza Recipe Description

lPizza is an Italian food that was created in the Naples area, Southern Italy. It is made with different toppings.
Some of the most common toppings are mozzarella (a kind of cheese), sausages, pepperoni, vegetables, tomatoes, spices and herbs and basil.
These toppings are added over a piece of bread covered with sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bacon
  • Meat

Steps to prepare

  1. Preprare dough
  2. Add sauce and cheese
  3. Add toppings
  4. Bake and serve
